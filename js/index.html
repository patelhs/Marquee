    A Few Words About Us

    Contemporary Hotels Design an Impression… Partner for Performance Results

    Marquee Hospitality LLC is a modern contemporary hotel management company specializing in the Kansas City area. We were first established in 2012 in order to bring multiple entities under one management company. Principals Mike and Nav Patel bring over 40 years of combined experience in the hotel development and management industry.

    Our company prides itself on being part of the day to day events of hotel management and adding a personal approach to our business. This consultative hands-on management style is evident in our product concept, our design and development, and our asset management strategy.

    Our company is especially focused on developing strong, long-term relationships with our clients. We partner with clients to envision and then build a successful long-range strategy that is mutually beneficial for everyone. This partnership adds tremendous value and helps our clients successfully achieve their business goals and performance results.

  • Nav Patel


    Nav Patel, Principal and Chief Financial Officer, moved to the U.S. at the age of 10 and grew up in the hotel business, helping his parents with just about every aspect of the business at the independent hotel they lived in and operated in Waynesboro, Virginia. In college, he studied pharmacy at VCU/MCV in Richmond, Virginia, and returned home during summers to help out with the business. Nav learned a lot of values working at the independent hotel, including the hard work and commitment needed to operate a hotel.

    Two years after graduating, with a Bachelor of Science in Pharmacy, he became more involved in the hotel business. He eventually helped design, manage the construction, and plan the grand opening of a Holiday Inn Express in Waynesboro, VA. This hotel enjoyed much success and was the winner of three Torchbearer awards.

    Nav continues to practice pharmacy today while also managing his hotel business. In 2012, he partnered with Mike, to develop and manage hotels in the Kansas City metro area. Nav oversees all the finances, creates budgets and projections, participates in revenue calls, and manages the site selection for potential new projects for the company.

  • Mike Patel


    Mike Patel, Principal and Chief Operations Officer, moved to the U.S. in 1982, and grew up in his parents’ motel business. He also had experience in franchised hotels, and independent hotels. He learned the business from the bottom up and worked in every department. He was able to grow his skills in maintenance and renovation as well as full-fledged construction and design.

    Mike graduated from the University of Missouri with a Computer Science and Finance Degree. After graduating, he handled the finances and day to day requirements of his parents’ motel. He also has experience managing and operating a Quiznos Sub Shop in Overland Park, KS. In 2005, Mike left the restaurant business to pursue his hotel business full time. He was excited to partner with brother-in-law Nav in 2012 to form Marquee Hospitality in the Kansas City area.

    Mike is closely involved in the day to day on site operations of the company. He works directly with site selection and designer development. He also enjoys building close relationships with franchise representatives and financial institutions. He was an Ambassador for the Intercontinental Hotel Group Owners Association from 2008-2011. He is also a current member of the Commerce Bank Advisory Board, the Missouri Kansas Hotel Owners Association and the Asian American Hotel Owners Association.

  • What We Do

    Stand out with trendy outside the box concepts that attract results

    Marquee Hospitality successfully helps hotels grow by being actively involved in the day to day operations of the hotel.

    We pride ourselves on our personal approach for a personal business. Our proactive guest service culture leads to better business, including better profitability, and sustainable long-term growth for our hotels. Our areas of expertise include:
    - Developing our owner and fee-managed hotels for a proven return on investment
    - Designing contemporary hotel space in our nationally recognized program
    - Recognizing industry cycles and trends and aligning properties to maximize returns and control expenses.
    - Partnering with other companies in the industry for a collaborative approach

    Our Mission

    Excellence, Integrity and Empowerment Set off our Highest Standards

    Marquee Hospitality is committed to consistently providing an excellent guest experience at a great value in our hotels. We aim for each guest to receive excellent service, amenities exceeding their expectations and high quality hotel rooms at affordable prices. We value integrity and being true to our word in all of our actions. We also foster a productive and positive working environment as well as the opportunity for consistent growth in our company. By creating an atmosphere of teamwork and open communication we empower our employees. As we grow, opportunities will open for employees to grow with us.
